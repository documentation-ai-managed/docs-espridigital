## Overview

Start organizing your docs by making a new project.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Projects">
    Click on "Projects" in the main sidebar after logging in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New">
    Click the "New Project" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Details">
    Fill in your project name and description. Choose visibility (public/private).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish">
    Click "Create". You can now add documentation to your new project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
