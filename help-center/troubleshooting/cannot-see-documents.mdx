---
title: Cannot See My Documents
description: Quick steps if your documents are missing in Espridigital.
---

## Symptoms

- Project dashboard is empty
- Documents not visible after login

## Common Causes

- Not added to the project
- Incorrect filters selected

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Project Access">
    Ensure you're added as a member on the project. Ask the admin if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Filters">
    Reset all filters using the "Clear Filters" button at the top of the documents list.
  </Step>
</Steps>
